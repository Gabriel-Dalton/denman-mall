Store Directory

Denman Place Mall is a landmark community hub in Vancouver’s West End, just steps from English Bay. Home to No Frills, Shoppers Drug Mart, Specsavers, Dollarama & 25+ local shops and services. Opened in 1969 as part of Canada’s first “vertical neighbourhood,” the mall continues to deliver convenient, service-focused retail daily from 8 AM to 10 PM.

Denman Place Mall FAQs

Where is Denman Place Mall located?

Denman Place Mall is conveniently located at 1030 Denman Street, Vancouver, BC, V6G 2M6 — just steps from English Bay.

What are Denman Place Mall’s hours of operation?

Most shops at Denman Place Mall operate Monday to Sunday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Some services and restaurants may have extended hours — please check directly with the business.

Is parking available at Denman Place Mall?

Paid parking is available through the Nelson Street entrance to the above-ground Diamond Service Parking Lot. It is independently operated and not affiliated with Denman Place Mall. Metered street parking is also available nearby, offering convenient options for visitors.

Are there public restrooms at Denman Place Mall?

Yes. Public washrooms are available daily from 10:30 AM to 5:30 PM. A key from a merchant is required. Washrooms may temporarily close for maintenance.
