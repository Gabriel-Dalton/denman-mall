Key numbers to know

  • Emergency (police, fire, ambulance) Call 911
  • Vancouver Police non-emergency 604-717-3321
  • HealthLink BC (24/7 nurse line) Call 8-1-1
  • Poison Control (BC) 1-800-567-8911
  • Mall enquiries Contact form

If you are on-site and need help, speak with mall security or a nearby retailer. They can direct you to the nearest exit, first-aid kit, or accessible route.

If there is a fire alarm

• Leave by the nearest marked exit. Do not use elevators.

• Follow staff and posted directions to assembly points outside.

• Do not re-enter the building until cleared by emergency services.

If you witness a medical emergency

• Call 911 first. Stay on the line with the dispatcher.

• Alert mall security or any retailer so staff can guide responders in.

• Do not move the person unless they are in immediate danger.

If you see suspicious activity

• If you feel in danger, call 911.

• Otherwise call VPD non-emergency: 604-717-3321.

• Note the location, time and description. Do not confront anyone.

If you are asked to shelter in place

• Move into the nearest store or back-of-house area away from windows.

• Silence your phone and follow staff direction.

• Wait for an all-clear from emergency services or staff.

Stay informed

For wider Vancouver or BC-area emergencies, the following sources publish verified, real-time information.

• City of Vancouver — Emergency Preparedness

• Vancouver Police Department

• Alertable — public emergency alerts

• HealthLink BC

This page is informational. It is not a substitute for calling 911 or following the directions of emergency responders and on-site staff.
