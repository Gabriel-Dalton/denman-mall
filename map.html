Mall Map & Floor Plan

Denman Place Mall sits on the corner of Denman and Comox Streets in Vancouver’s West End, a short walk from English Bay and Stanley Park. Use the guide below to find your way around.

Entrances

Denman Street (main)

Getting Here

By car, on foot, by bike, or by transit — see our Parking & Transit page for details on the adjacent parking lot, bus routes serving Denman Street, and the walk from Burrard SkyTrain Station.

Accessibility

The ground floor is step-free from the Denman Street entrance, and the elevator connects to the lower level. See our Accessibility Statement for full details and how to request assistance.
